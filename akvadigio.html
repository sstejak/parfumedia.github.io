Today's picks

Today’s top-selling perfumes combine timeless elegance with modern innovation.
These fragrances captivate with their unique blends and broad appeal, making them favorites worldwide.

Aqua di Gio

Acqua di Gio by Giorgio Armani is a beloved fragrance inspired by the Mediterranean’s natural beauty. Known for its fresh, aquatic notes blended with citrus, herbs, and a hint of woody warmth, it evokes a sense of freedom and sophistication. Versatile and timeless, it’s a favorite for both everyday wear and special occasions.

Musc Noir

Musc Noir by Narciso Rodriguez is a sensual and elegant fragrance that highlights the depth of musk combined with sweet plum and hints of heliotrope. This scent exudes mystery and sophistication, making it perfect for intimate settings.

Afternoon Swim

Afternoon Swim by Louis Vuitton is a fresh and invigorating fragrance that captures the essence of a carefree summer day. With vibrant citrus notes like orange, bergamot, and mandarin, it offers a refreshing and uplifting experience.

Armani My Way

My Way by Giorgio Armani is a modern floral fragrance that celebrates discovery and connection. It features notes of orange blossom, tuberose, and warm vanilla, creating a bright and feminine profile.

Tom Ford Ombre Leather

Ombre Leather by Tom Ford is a bold and distinctive fragrance with rich leather at its core. Enhanced by notes of jasmine and cardamom, it offers a smooth, smoky, and luxurious scent experience.

Yves Saint Laurent Libre

Libre by Yves Saint Laurent is a sophisticated blend of floral and warm oriental notes. With lavender, orange blossom, and vanilla, it strikes a perfect balance between boldness and femininity.

Le Male Le Parfum

Le Male Le Parfum by Jean Paul Gaultier is an intense and refined version of the original Le Male. With notes of cardamom, lavender, and vanilla, it delivers a rich, spicy, and seductive aroma.

Black Opium

Black Opium by Yves Saint Laurent is a bold and addictive fragrance that combines the warmth of coffee with the sweetness of vanilla and orange blossom. It’s a captivating scent for those who love a touch of mystery and glamour.
