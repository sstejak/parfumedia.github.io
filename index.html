Francis Kurkdjian

Francis Kurkdjian, born in Paris in 1969, is a renowned Armenian perfumer and businessman, awarded the Prix François Coty in 2001 for his lifetime achievement. He gained fame at 26 for creating Jean Paul Gaultier's iconic Le Male and went on to design over 40 fragrances for top brands, including Elie Saab Le Parfum, My Burberry, and Narciso Rodriguez for Her.

Rodrigo Flores-Roux

Rodrigo Flores-Roux, born in Mexico, moved to France in 1989 to study at ISIPCA in Versailles and interned with Jean-Claude Ellena at Givaudan. He began his career creating scents for brands like Calvin Klein and Elizabeth Arden, and later made his first fine fragrance for Clinique. Now a Senior Perfumer at Givaudan, he joined the company in 2007.

Luca Maffei

Luca Maffei is a young Italian independent perfumer and founder of Atelier Fragranze Milano. Since 2014, he has been a member of the Société Française des Parfumeurs and has created over 50 fragrances for both mainstream and niche brands, including air scents and candles. Passionate about essences from childhood, he won the Art and Olfaction Awards for his creations.
